# 18-Conversation Eval Batch — Report

**Run date:** 2026-04-30
**Wall clock:** 12 min (4 students concurrent)
**Total cost:** $9.24
**Sessions:** 18 (11 students: 3 pairs + 2 triples + 6 solos)

---

## TL;DR

**System mechanics (Changes 1–5.1) are working correctly.** Memory cycle, gate, counter system, mastery scoring all behave as designed. Most dimensional scores are healthy:
- ARC mean **0.914** (Change 3+4 working)
- TLQ **0.89**, RRQ **0.81**, AQ **0.84**, PP **0.81**, TRQ **0.81**
- RAGAS faithfulness **0.99**, EULER question_present **1.00**

**But the eval framework's `failed_critical_penalty` verdict fires on all 18 sessions.** Most penalties (110/155) are `INVARIANT_VIOLATION` — one per ungrounded tutoring turn. Long-tail timeout sessions accumulate 14–19 each. **The eval framework was calibrated for short gate-test sessions and is too punitive on full-flow sessions.** Penalty design needs adjustment (count violations once per session, not per-turn).

**Real product concerns surfaced:**
1. EULER `helpful` mean **0.61** (below 0.70 threshold) — Socratic responses not always advancing reasoning
2. RAGAS `context_precision` mean **0.36** (below 0.80) — retrieval noisy
3. EULER `no_reveal` mean **0.89** (below 0.95) — answer leaks ~10% of turns
4. **Lock-quality drift**: 6/18 sessions had sentence-form `locked_answer` (≥6 words); 1/18 locked on completely wrong concept

## 2. Lock-quality drift — REAL BUG

| Session | Topic asked | locked_answer produced | Issue |
|---|---|---|---|
| pair3_S5 s1 | "phases of the cardiac cycle" | `'latent period contraction phase relaxation phase'` | **Wrong concept** (those are muscle-twitch phases) + sentence-form |
| triple1_S3 s1 | "parts of a nephron" | `'renal corpuscle proximal convoluted tubule loop of henle distal convoluted tubule'` | 11 words — list, not anchor |
| triple1_S3 s3 | "tubular reabsorption" | `'active transport diffusion facilitated diffusion secondary active transport osmosis'` | 9 words — list |
| triple2_S6 s3 | "small intestine absorption" | `'active transport passive diffusion facilitated diffusion co transport endocytosis'` | 9 words — list |
| solo2_S2 | "controls breathing rate in brainstem" | `'medulla oblongata and pontine respiratory group'` | 6 words — borderline |
| **solo3_S3** | **"structure of a long bone"** | **`'skeletal cardiac and smooth muscle'`** | **CRITICAL — locked on completely wrong concept (muscle types, not bone structure)** |

**Recommendation:** the `_sanitize_locked_answer` rule of "2–10 words, hard max 15" is being violated by the LLM. The recent prompt update tightened this to "2-5 words, hard max 10" (Change 1's repair) but the lock LLM is still producing list-form answers. **Tighten the lock prompt further** OR add a deterministic post-check that rejects answers with ≥3 commas / ≥3 conjunctions.

| **OFF_TOPIC_DRIFT_NOT_REDIRECTED** | 1 | Major | Single off-topic in pair3_s2 |

**Most actionable: INVARIANT_VIOLATION mass-firing. This is an eval-framework calibration issue, not a system-under-test bug.** Fix: in `evaluation/quality/penalties.py`, count `INVARIANT_VIOLATION` as ONE penalty per session (severity=critical) rather than one per violation entry.

## 7. Recommendations (in priority order)

### Critical (fix before deploy)

1. **Fix lock-quality drift** — solo3_S3 locked on muscle types when asked about long bones. Investigate `_lock_anchors_call` for strict topic anchoring. Add deterministic post-check rejecting locked_answers with ≥3 commas or list-form syntax.
2. **Investigate `LEAK_DETECTED` (10 events)** — sample 2–3 of them and tighten the `_quality_check_call` no_reveal threshold OR teacher_socratic prompt to reduce reveals.
3. **Fix `INVARIANT_VIOLATION` over-counting in eval framework** — one penalty per session, not per turn. `evaluation/quality/penalties.py` line where invariant violations are appended needs dedup.

### Major (post-deploy)

4. **Improve EULER `helpful` (0.61)** — the deferred dimensional-hint work directly addresses this. Hints currently progressive-directness; should target different dimensions (structure/function/mechanism/analogy/clinical).
5. **Fix RAGAS `context_precision` (0.36)** — cross-encoder reranking exists but isn't filtering aggressively enough. Tune the cutoff threshold.
6. **Investigate S6 suspiciously-fast reach pattern** — 4/4 sessions in 2 turns. Either simulator over-confident or gate too lenient on exploratory phrasings.
7. **Mem0 read failures on pair1, pair2 session 2** — sometimes mem0 returns nothing. Debug and add fallback narration.
8. **CE cost target re-calibration** — $0.20/turn is too tight; bump to $0.50/turn with note about long-session amortization.
