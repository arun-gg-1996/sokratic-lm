## Analysis

Gate 1 fails on count, but the **length distribution is the best of all three thresholds**:

- mean=531 chars (~133 words) — closest to DPR-style 100-word retrieval passages
- median=394 chars — very focused, factual chunks
- max=5,199 chars — max is dramatically lower than threshold=95's max of 16,649
- 85% of chunks fall at or below 900 chars

At threshold=75, the splitter cuts at the 25% most dissimilar sentence transitions.
This is aggressive but produces high-precision, topic-focused chunks — exactly what
dense retrieval needs for factual Q&A.

**Post-propositions estimate:**
4,032 para chunks × ~3 propositions (shorter chunks → fewer props per chunk)
≈ ~12,000 propositions. This may be too many for a first version but is
architecturally valid if Qdrant can handle it.
