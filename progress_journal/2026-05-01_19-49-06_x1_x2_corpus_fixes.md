## Bug X1 — Chapter 20 entirely missing from `data/topic_index.json`

### Root cause (specific)

`textbook_structure.json:1192` has the key:

```
"Chapter 20: The Cardiovascular System: Blood Vessels and Circulation"
```

After `_strip_chapter_prefix`, that becomes
`"The Cardiovascular System: Blood Vessels and Circulation"`.

But **439 chunks for chapter 20 had `chapter_title='Circulation'`** —
the chunker truncated the multi-colon title to just the last word.
Diff (audited via cross-comparison of unique chapter titles in chunks
vs structure):

```
Mismatches (in structure but not in chunks):
  'The Cardiovascular System: Blood Vessels and Circulation'

Extra in chunks but not in structure:
  'Circulation'  (chapter_num=[20])
  'REFERENCES'   (chapter_num=[28])
```

`scripts/build_topic_index.py::_count_chunks` keys on `chapter_title`
verbatim. With chunk-side title `'Circulation'` and structure-side
title `'The Cardiovascular System: Blood Vessels and Circulation'`,
the join produced 0 matches. `_aggregate_counts(...)` returned 0 for
every ch20 subsection → all 26 ch20 subsections dropped by the
`min_chunk_count >= 1` filter → ch20 invisible to TopicMatcher /
sample_related / coverage-gate.

Bonus finding: 12 chunks tagged `chapter_title='REFERENCES'` are
bibliography pages from the back-matter that snuck through PDF
extraction. Not real content — should be dropped.

### Fix

`scripts/fix_corpus_metadata_2026_05_01.py` — applied to JSONL + Qdrant
in lockstep:

| Action | Count |
|---|---|
| `chapter_title='Circulation'` → `'The Cardiovascular System: Blood Vessels and Circulation'` (ch20 chunks) | 439 |
| Drop `chapter_title='REFERENCES'` chunks (bibliography junk) | 12 |
| Subsection truncation (`Diseases of the…`) interim rename — superseded by drop, see X2 | 6 |

For each affected chunk: rewrite the JSONL entry AND `client.set_payload`
on the matching Qdrant point so the retriever returns corrected
metadata. `client.delete` on REFERENCES points (12 removed from Qdrant).

Backup at `data/processed/chunks_openstax_anatomy.jsonl.pre_2026_05_01.bak`
for rollback safety. Script idempotent (re-runs find nothing to fix).

### Schema migration discovered while rebuilding

`scripts/build_topic_index.py` had THREE other latent bugs blocking
the rebuild:

1. **Wrong chunks file**: hardcoded fallback to `chunks_ot.jsonl`
   (propositions-era artifact, no longer present). Fixed: prefer
   `cfg.paths.chunks_openstax_anatomy`.
2. **Stale chunk-schema parsing**: `_count_chunks` expected
   `section_title` to encode subsection via `" — "` em-dash join
   (legacy schema). Current schema has separate `subsection_title`
   field. Result: every subsection-level vote was collapsed to
   section-level → 9 topics returned (out of 363+ expected). Fixed:
   prefer `subsection_title` field when present, fall back to em-dash
   split for legacy-format chunks.
3. **Missing `chapter_num`**: prior pipeline relied on a one-off
   backfill script. Made `chapter_num` a first-class field in
   `build_topic_index.py` extracted directly from the structure key
   via `CHAPTER_NUM_RE`.

## Bug X2 — 6 chunks with truncated `subsection_title='Diseases of the…'`

### Root cause

`textbook_structure.json` has 46 callout-box entries truncated to
`'Disorders of the...'` or `'Aging and the...'` (PDF parse artifacts —
the callouts have variable per-chapter titles like "Disorders of the
Heart and Vascular Health" or "Aging and the Cardiovascular System"
that the parser failed to capture in full). Six matching chunks have
slightly different truncations: `'Diseases of the…'` (literal ellipsis
char `…`).

These callouts are NOT teachable subsections — they're pedagogical
sidebars. The structure already routes them through the junk-pattern
filter at `config/domains/ot.yaml:60-66`:

```yaml
junk_patterns:
  - "DISORDERS OF THE"
  - "AGING AND THE"
```

So the structure-side entries are correctly dropped during build. But
the 6 chunks were orphans — present in JSONL + Qdrant, never
referenceable by any topic-index entry.

### Fix

`scripts/drop_disorders_callout_chunks_2026_05_01.py` — drops the 6
callout chunks from JSONL + Qdrant entirely. Cleaner than renaming
because callout content has no place in the teachable surface.

```
Input chunks:  7562
Drop count:    6
Output chunks: 7556
Qdrant 'sokratic_kb_chunks' was 7562 → 7556 points (deleted 6)
```
